Butternut squash soup

Description

The butternut squash soup is an ideal, hot, hearty meal during the frosty winter months. The
recipe only requires a few ingredients, however you will need an immersion blender. Thankfully,
you may obtain one at an appliance store or on Amazon for a very reasonable price.

Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash
  • 2 big carrots
  • 1 big onion
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Handful of toasted pumpkin seeds
  • Cayenne pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon of sour cream

Steps

  1. Slice the onion and cook it in a pot with some olive oil and a pinch of salt at medium-low heat until it starts to caramelize.
  2. Meanwhile, remove the seeds and skin from the squash and dice it into small cubes.
  3. Roughly chop the carrots.
  4. Once the onion is soft and slightly brown, add the chopped squash and carrots to the pot.
  5. Add water until it barely submerges the vegetables and cook at high heat for 20-30min.
  6. Once the vegetables are tender, use the immersion blender to blend the ingredients together until a smooth consistency.
